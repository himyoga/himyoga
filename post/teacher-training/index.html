Yoga Teacher Training

200 Hours Yoga Teacher Training

YOGA TRAINING learn yoga in the HIMALAYAN YOGA offer comprehensive Ashtanga Yoga, Hatha Yoga Teacher training programs for 200 hours, in dharamshala, Mcleodganj. Himalayan yoga is registered with government of india. The Yoga teacher training courses in India are in-depth and follow the guidelines as listed by HIMALAYAN YOGA.

Yoga is known for its therapeutic benefits.At Himalayan Yoga, We teach the therapeutic style of yoga and Himalayan yoga History. In this style, the course begins with the health check up of every students who have some physical troubles and special asanas are taught to them to overcomes their problems All the necessary equioment, such as mats, blocks,belts, chairs, stools, bolsters, blankets etc.required during asanas is provided with in the campus.The teaching style is simple and personal.

Our teacher understand that yoga is not easy cant be a hurried effort and demands commitment.They are patient and empathetic, helping students overcome their fears to savor the joys of yoga.

The daily Schedule includes morning asanas and pranayama followed by flow yoga in the afternoon. Flow yoga in the afternoon flow yoga in the afternoon flow yoga teacher students how different asanas can be sequenced. An expertise in yoga doesn’t automatically translate into teaching, yoga is a special skill which goes beyond asanas.Therefore special attention is paid to thjis aspect of the courses also after all most students who take up these courses in yoga. Apart from teaching deeper levels of yoga, classes are held for teaching students are taught about the yoga touch and traits of patience and empathy are evoked in students so that tomorrow they are able to teach all kinds of students.

At HIMALAYAN YOGA We also introduce our students to Ayurveda (sanskrit word literally translated as knowledge about the life) Both Yoga and Ayurveda go hand in hand and together bring about unforeseen benefits to the human body. Students also learn how to do Trataka or yogic gazing, satsang a process of concentrating on an object to allow the ever wandering thoughts toa come to a still, bringing peace to the mind apart from providing immense ocular and psychological benefits.

You can pay by credit card, by paypal (commission applies), by bank transfer, or cash in Indian currency.
